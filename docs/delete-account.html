Delete your Citysnap account

App: Citysnap – local city thoughts, snaps & conversations
Developer contact: citysnap.play.review@gmail.com

You can permanently delete your Citysnap account and associated data directly in the app, or by emailing us if you cannot access the app.

Delete your account in the app (recommended)

  1. Open the Citysnap app on your Android device.
  2. Sign in to the account you want to delete.
  3. Tap Profile in the bottom navigation bar.
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete account.
  5. Read the confirmation message, then confirm deletion.
  6. If prompted, re-enter your password or sign in with Google again to verify it is you.
This action is permanent. Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered.

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, Citysnap permanently removes:

  • Your profile (name, email, city, locality, and profile photo)
  • All posts (thoughts) you created
  • All comments you posted
  • Photos (snaps) you uploaded
  • Your “I feel this” reactions
  • Saved posts, notification preferences, blocked users list, and other account settings
  • Your Firebase Authentication login for Citysnap

What data may be kept

  • Moderation reports you filed may be retained for a limited time to investigate abuse and meet legal obligations. These are not shown on your profile after deletion.
  • Crash and analytics data collected by Firebase Crashlytics and Analytics may be retained in anonymized or aggregated form according to Google’s retention policies (typically up to a few months).
  • Backups on our service providers may take up to 30 days to be fully purged after deletion.

Delete some data without deleting your account

You do not need to delete your whole account to remove specific content:

  • Delete a post: open your post → tap Delete (from Profile or the post menu).
  • Delete a comment: open the post → tap Delete on your comment.

Cannot access the app?

Email citysnap.play.review@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account. Include your display name and city. We will verify ownership and process your deletion request within 30 days.
